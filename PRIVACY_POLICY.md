# Privacy Policy
Privacy Policy for Halaqa Passport app

## What information is collected?
We collect a participant nickname, an app-generated participant ID, an age band, and timestamps for when a participant profile is created and last seen. We also store attendance records that include participant ID, session ID, series ID, and a check-in timestamp.

## Why is this information collected?
This data is used to create a participant profile, prevent duplicate check-ins, and show attendance history, streaks, and badges inside the app.

## Do we use cookies?
No cookies are used at this time.

## Does the app include third-party analytics?
No.

## Does the app include third-party advertising?
No.

## Will the data be shared with any third parties?
We use Firebase (Firestore and Cloud Functions) to store and process app data. We do not sell or share data with third parties for advertising.

## Is the app collecting any user or device data for purposes beyond third-party analytics or third-party advertising?
No.
